🧪 Alchemy Basics

Alchemy in Kingdom Come: Deliverance 2 is a hands-on, immersive system that rewards attention to detail. You'll be mixing herbs, timing boils, and grinding ingredients like a medieval chemist.

Unlike simplified crafting systems in many RPGs, KCD2's alchemy requires physically performing each step of the brewing process.

  • Alchemy Benches: Found in most towns and monasteries.
  • Potion Quality: Ranges from weak to exceptional, depending on precision.
  • Perks: Unlock better efficiency and results.

🌿 Gathering Ingredients

You can find potion ingredients by:

  • Foraging: Explore the world and harvest herbs.
  • Buying: Visit apothecaries and herbalists.

Different regions yield different herbs. Look for specific plants in their natural habitats: riverside for water-loving herbs, forests for mushrooms, and meadows for flowers.

Gathering Perks

  • Flower Power: +2 Charisma with 30+ herbs.
  • Leg Day: Strength XP bonus when picking herbs.
  • Lucky Find: Increased chances for treasure/herb finds.

📖 Understanding Recipes

Use the recipe book at alchemy benches to see required ingredients and steps.

Learn recipes in three different ways, each with its own advantages and challenges:

  • Buy: From vendors.
  • Find: Hidden throughout the world.
  • Experiment: Try your own combinations (high risk, high reward).

Recipe books will show you the exact quantities, base liquids, and procedures needed. Pay close attention to the order of operations—it matters!

⚗️ Brewing Process

Each potion follows a series of interactive steps:

  1. Select a base liquid (Water, Wine, Spirits, Oil).
  2. Measure and add ingredients (some need grinding).
  3. Boil ingredients using the sandglass.
  4. Use bellows to intensify the heat (if needed).
  5. Finish by pouring or distilling.

Timing and accuracy matter—slip-ups ruin the brew. Practice makes perfect, and higher Alchemy skill grants more forgiveness for minor mistakes.

For complex potions, you may need to phlebotomize (draw blood), calcinate (burn to ash), or distill multiple times. The workbench provides all the tools you need.

🧠 Alchemy Perks

As you level up your Alchemy skill, you'll unlock powerful perks that make brewing easier and more rewarding:

  • Secret of Secrets: Brew exceptional potions with better effects.
  • Secret of Matter: Get an extra potion per brew.
  • Secret of Equilibrium: Forgiving minor mistakes during brewing.

These perks represent Henry's growing mastery of the alchemical arts. With enough practice, you'll be able to create potions that significantly tip combat and survival in your favor.

🧴 Sample Potion Recipes

These basic recipes will get you started on your alchemical journey:

Aesop

  • Base: Spirits
  • Ingredients: Belladonna x1, Boar's Tusk x1, Comfrey x2
  • Effect: Boosts riding and dog-handling. Animals ignore you more when brewed well.

Aqua Vitalis

  • Base: Water
  • Ingredients: Dandelion x2, Marigold x1
  • Effect: Slows bleeding and reduces health loss.

Artemisia

  • Base: Spirits
  • Ingredients: Wormwood x2, Sage x1
  • Effect: +2 Strength for 10 minutes.
